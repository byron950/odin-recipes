Steak and Potatoes

Delicious and Nutritious steak and Potatoes!

This is a simple recipe but you can never go wrong with it! It always tastes good and it is highly Nutritious, perfect for bulking!!

Ingredients:

  • Steak
  • Potato (Or sweet potato)
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celcius
  2. Cover the potatoes in melted butter
  3. Add salt and pepper then let the potatoes bake for 0h50m - 1h15m
  4. Cook the steak until medium rare (anything more than that is not allowed for this meal!)
  5. ENJOY!!
